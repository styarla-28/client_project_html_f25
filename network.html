Building Connection and Networking!

Build Your Network, Build Your Future

Networking is a crucial skill in today's job market, and building meaningful connections can open doors to opportunities you might not find otherwise. Whether you're looking to connect with alumni, industry professionals, or peers, this section provides the resources and strategies you need to expand your network effectively.

Connect. Grow. Succeed

At UMSI, we believe in the power of relationships. That's why we've gathered tips, tools, and best practices to help you navigate networking events, leverage social media platforms like LinkedIn, and cultivate long-lasting professional relationships. Start building your network today and take a proactive step toward your career goals.

Why Does Networking Matter?

  • Access Hidden Opportunities: Up to 80% of jobs are filled through referrals and personal connections.
  • Learn from Others: Tap into the experience and insights of professionals in your field.
  • Build Confidence: Practicing networking boosts your communication skills and self-assurance.
  • Create a Support System: Build relationships with mentors, peers, and collaborators who can guide you through every stage of your career.

How to Start Networking

  1. Attend Events: Join us for workshops, career fairs, alumni panels, and employer meet-and-greets—virtually or on campus.
  2. Leverage Social Media: Build a strong LinkedIn profile, join professional groups, and engage in conversations in your field.
  3. Prepare Your Elevator Pitch: Have a concise introduction ready that highlights your skills and career goals.
  4. Follow Up: Send personalized thank-you notes or LinkedIn connection requests after meeting someone new.
  5. Get Involved: Volunteer with campus organizations, industry associations, or community groups to expand your network organically.

Connect with UofM Alumni!

UMSI alumni are a valuable resource for networking and career advice. Use the U-M Alumni Association's Alumni Directory to find and connect with alumni in your field of interest. Attend alumni events and join UMSI's LinkedIn alumni group to expand your network further.
